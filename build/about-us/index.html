Who We Are

30 Years in the Trade. One Electrician on Every Job.

Mike DiNinno is the owner and operator of Mike DiNinno Electric, LLC. A native of Ringwood, New Jersey, Mike graduated from DePaul High School in Wayne, NJ in 1981 and went on to serve his country as a U.S. Navy veteran, receiving an Honorable Discharge in 1987.

Mike began his electrical career in the Navy, receiving training in Basic Electronics, Electricity, and Electronic Technology. After his service, he continued his training at Passaic County Vocational School in Residential, Commercial, and Industrial electrical systems, and has never stopped learning since.

From apprentice to wireman to journeyman to supervisory roles, Mike worked his way up through the trade over decades before earning his New Jersey Electrical Contractor's License in 2013. That same year, he launched Mike DiNinno Electric, LLC.

Our Roots, Our Purpose, Our Future

History · Mission · Vision

Mike DiNinno Electric was built on a foundation of experience, integrity, and hands-on service. Mike began his electrical career in the U.S. Navy as an Electronic Technician, then continued his training in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. After decades in the field and earning his Electrical Contractor's License, he launched Mike DiNinno Electric, LLC in 2013. With over 30 years of experience, Mike brings a personal touch to every project, treating each home or business with care, precision, and respect.

At Mike DiNinno Electric, our mission is to provide dependable, high-quality electrical work with a personal touch. We believe every client deserves honest service, clear communication, and results that exceed expectations. From small residential jobs to complex commercial projects, we approach every task with care, professionalism, and a commitment to doing it right the first time. Our goal is simple: deliver safe, clean, and reliable electrical solutions that earn our customers' trust for life.

Our vision is to be the most trusted name in electrical services throughout New Jersey. We aim to grow through lasting relationships, referrals, and a reputation for excellence built one job at a time. By combining decades of hands-on experience with ongoing training and modern solutions, we're committed to staying at the forefront of our industry while never losing the personal, owner-operated approach that sets us apart.
