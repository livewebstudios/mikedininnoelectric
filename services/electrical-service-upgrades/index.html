Electrical Service Upgrades & Panel Replacements in NJ

Licensed. Insured. NJ code-compliant, every job.

⚡ Electrical Emergency? Call Now · 973.787.7122

Your electrical panel is the backbone of your home or business's power supply. If it was installed decades ago, it likely wasn't designed for today's electrical demands, EV chargers, smart home devices, whole-house generators, and multiple high-draw appliances can push an older panel to its limits.

Mike DiNinno Electric performs residential and commercial service upgrades from 100A to 200A and beyond, replacing outdated panels, fuse boxes, and undersized service equipment with modern, code-compliant systems that are safe, reliable, and ready for what's next.

Signs You Need an Upgrade

  • Panel is more than 25 years old
  • You still have a fuse box
  • Breakers trip frequently
  • Lights dim or flicker when appliances run
  • You've added an EV charger, AC unit, or major appliance
  • Planning a home addition or renovation

What's Included

  • Full panel assessment and load calculation
  • Utility coordination
  • Permit filing and inspection scheduling
  • Professional installation with full cleanup
  • Post-install testing and walkthrough
