What Is a Service Contract Agreement?

A Service Contract Agreement (SCA) with Mike DiNinno Electric gives your business or facility the benefits of an on-staff electrician, without the payroll overhead. It's an economical, efficient way to manage your electrical needs, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

What You Get

Priority Response

Your service calls move to the front of the line, around the clock.

Fixed Pricing

Guaranteed fixed costs and fixed markup on materials. No surprises on your invoice.

Energy Audits

Periodic energy efficiency reviews with recommendations to reduce consumption and operating costs.

Free Walk-Throughs

Scheduled facility walk-throughs to identify potential problem areas before they become emergencies.

Single Point of Contact

One number. One team. All your electrical needs handled.

24/7 Availability

True around-the-clock service for SCA clients. We answer when you call.

Who This Is For

Commercial, industrial, and multi-unit residential property owners and facility managers who need dependable electrical service without managing multiple vendor relationships.
