Meet the Owner

Mike DiNinno

U.S. Navy veteran. 30+ years in the electrical trade. Licensed NJ Electrical Contractor since 2013. Mike works every job himself — he's not sending a crew. You get the owner, every time.

After serving in the Navy as an Electronic Technician and training at Passaic County Vocational School, Mike has built a reputation across New Jersey for clean work, clear communication, and an obsessive attention to detail. Customers routinely say he left their home cleaner than he found it.
